Quality Assurance

Our Quality Standards

Rigorous testing and validation at every stage to ensure system reliability.

Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT)

Comprehensive testing of all system components at our facility before deployment. Includes functional tests, integration verification, and performance validation against specifications.

Site Acceptance Testing (SAT)

On-site validation of installed systems in their actual environment. Includes integration with existing infrastructure, user acceptance testing, and operational readiness verification.

Certification & Compliance

All systems delivered in compliance with international standards including ISO 9001, ISO 27001, ISO 45001, and NFPA fire safety regulations.
