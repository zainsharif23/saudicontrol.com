Who We Are

Saudi Controls Ltd. (SCL) is a leading Engineering & Systems Integration company based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Established in 1997, we bring 25+ years of experience delivering world-class solutions across the Kingdom.

With 300+ completed projects, 200+ long-term clients, and a team of 65+ employees, we specialize in Building Management Systems (BMS), CCTV & Security, Industrial Automation, Intelligent Traffic Systems (ITS), Access Control, Fire Alarm, Communication & AV Systems, and IoT Solutions.

Under the leadership of our CEO & Chairman, Mr. Abdulrahman Abdullah Z. Al-Shathry, SCL continues to engineer the future of smart systems for Saudi Arabia and beyond.

Vision, Mission & Values

Our Vision

To be the leading engineering and systems integration partner in the Middle East, driving smart, sustainable, and connected infrastructure across every sector.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, reliable, and integrated engineering solutions that exceed client expectations while contributing to the Kingdom's Vision 2030 goals.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, innovation, and partnership guide every project we undertake — building trust through quality delivery and long-term relationships.
