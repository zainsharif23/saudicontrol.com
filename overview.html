A Trusted Systems Integration Partner Since 1997

Saudi Controls Ltd. (SCL) is a leading Engineering & Systems Integration company based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. With 25+ years of experience, 300+ completed projects, and 200+ long-term clients, we deliver mission-critical solutions for buildings, infrastructure, and industrial facilities.

Our Story

25 Years of Engineering Excellence

Our Journey

Founded in 1997 in Riyadh, Saudi Controls Ltd. began with a vision to provide world-class engineering and systems integration solutions to the Kingdom. Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a small team of engineers to a workforce of 65+ experts serving clients across Saudi Arabia and the region.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic partnerships with global technology leaders, and an unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. We have successfully delivered over 300 projects across diverse sectors including healthcare, education, government, industrial, and commercial facilities.

Today, we are recognized as a trusted partner for mission-critical systems integration, helping organizations operate safer, smarter, and more efficiently through integrated technology ecosystems.
